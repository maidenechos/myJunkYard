Harry Potter and the Arbitrary Sequel

Review date:
29th May 2013
Rating:
3¼

You know how the characters in Harry Potter are always banging on about "muggles", and how we're all so inadequate that we have to use things like "technology", to make up for not having magic..?

Well, wouldn't it be funny if it turned out that "magic" is actually heuristic nano-technology, sent back in time from the distant future to the middle-ages, and originally invented by us!

It'd be like, Ha! Who's the muggles now!
